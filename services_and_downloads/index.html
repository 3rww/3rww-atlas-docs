Sewer Atlas Data Downloads and Services

All Sewer Atlas data is available for download in multiple GIS formats on the MDS Downloads page.

Web geodata services endpoints—links to the live data through OGC-compliant service endpoints—are currently not available, but we definitely plan to make those available in the future through integration with 3RWW’s Single Sign-On system.
